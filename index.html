Welcome — I'm Emma Forman

I'm a builder and connector working at the intersection of food, technology, and community.

Whether it's launching AI tools at DoorDash Labs, creating space for female founders through Myca Collective, or bringing together the foodtech ecosystem via FoodHack NYC, I care about designing systems—and communities—that make the future of food more sustainable, collaborative, and alive.

Right now, my focus is on:

  • DoorDash Labs, where I help lead automation and robotics to power the future of commerce
  • Myca Collective, a community I founded for women reimagining food, climate, and agriculture
  • FoodHack NYC, where I organize events to connect founders, funders, and operators shaping the next generation of food systems

I believe food is our most tangible connection to the world around us—and the most powerful tool we have to drive change.
